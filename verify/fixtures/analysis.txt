We measured the average customer lifetime value by taking every customer who is
still active today and averaging how much each has spent with us. The mean came
out to $4,200, so we are telling the board that the typical customer is worth
about $4,200.

We left out the customers who churned, because their records were incomplete and
they would only have dragged the number down. We also compared this quarter's
signups to last quarter's, counting only the signups who are still active now.
